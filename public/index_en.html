The Dark Room is an air-conditioned playground for your neurons; a space where we sacrifice sight and enhance our other senses: touch, taste, smell, sound… but also anticipation, intimacy, adventure, connection, and self-discovery. Description

Mirage is a visual phenomenon, a lucid dream, a realistic atmospheric occurrence in which we will create a reflection, or multiple reflections, of tranquility. Mirage is derived from the Latin word mirari which means "to be amazed". Mirage is home, a place for family and friends, embracing and pleasant and definitely pampering. A place for rest and relaxation, a place to meet friends, new and newer, a shaded and welcoming camp, with a well-organized and rich kitchen, WC, shower and plenty of love to go around. An alternative zone within the camp, for relaxing, an inspiring place to dream, discover yourself, fulfill dreams and live the moment to its fullest. A place to create yourself and lovingly share surprises and gifting, pampering our guests in the desert. We also display at Camp Mirage a multi-screen video collage photo gallery from Midburn and Burning Man, taken by photographers from Israel and all around the world, who have come together to share and encapsulate magical moments of the Burner Community. The camp is limited to a number of friends, a pampering and fruitful group, motivated by giving, so if you feel like making your home in an oasis at the heart of the dream - you are invited to join with love and creativity
